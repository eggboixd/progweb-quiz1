Monas

Jakarta's Icon. The best place to find authentic Jakarta food and get the Jakarta street-life vibe.
Perpustakaan/Taman Ismail Marzuki

Great place to study or if you want to find a quiet hangout spot. Very aesthetically pleasing and perfect to get some work done and to refresh your mind at the park after.

A nice break from the concrete jungle of Jakarta. Tons of greenery and space to do outdoors activity.
